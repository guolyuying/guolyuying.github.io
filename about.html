About

Lyuying Guo

Hi! This is Lyuying, a nerdy artist. I am a 3D Rigger and Modeler with a technical understanding and curiosity in computer graphics.

Born and raised in mainland China, I moved to Los Angeles after graduating public high school to pursue my dream in animation at the University of Southern California (USC). Having completed an Art BA where I drew and painted on canvas every day, I am currently pursuing an MFA in Animation at the Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD) in Savannah, GA, focusing on the technical aspect of 3D animation. I use Maya and Houdini for most of my work, and am always learning new tools and techniques.

In my free time, I like to draw slice-of-life comics and write articles about my observations and thoughts on the world.
