Hi! My name is Lyuying. I am an animator and illustrator, currently an animation MFA student at the Savannah College of Art and Design with a focus on 3D animation.

Previously, I studied illustration and visual development as a fine art undergrad at the University of Southern California. I worked as a freelance illustrator and background artist upon graduation.

When I am not praticing animation and 3D modeling, I like to draw slice-of-life comics and write articles about my observation of the mundane world.
