About

Lyuying Guo

Hi! My name is Lyuying. I am an animator, illustrator, and currently an animation MFA student at the Savannah College of Art and Design with a focus on technical animation.

Previously, I was a fine art undergraduate student at the University of Southern California, focusing on illustration and visual development. I worked as a freelance illustrator and background artist for a year after graduating from USC.

When I am not praticing animation and modeling in Maya, I like to draw slice-of-life comics and write articles about my daily life, my thoughts on life, and my observation of the world around us.
